Lost in your dreams?
Well, No this not about me trying to promote the idea that Artwork is a way of rescue or some sort of escapism, but more like share my thoughts on how I would and like to highlight that fretting about A, obsessing over Z, yet forgetting all about B to Y is like feeling trapped in your own trap. There’s a room for everybody, nobody can do with your voice or probably here more like your hands and brain, So let it just flow, let it out, be proud of what you owe and keep learning.Art to me is a form of expressing myself in ways even I never knew off. And there’s certainly not one thing I can compare art with because it’s always caved unhackneyed ideas and perspective for me. I can certainly exemplify this by attaching a few artworks of mine, which show varied emotions and every picture has an untold story. There is no fixed genre that it emphasises. Each art highlights my mood, not really focusing on one notable area or idea.

The overwhelming feeling of having so many thoughts and ideas at once and not enough time to be able cultivate them, is something what this art speaks about. Similar to the journey of life, we have phases trying to embraces all the chaos, trying to battle so many things, we baffle, but proceed with great changes. The circle of ocean blue colour or the way I like to see is the full circle of life and emotions, illustrates the phases of emotions when feeling swamped, it talks through the language of water and the connection of situations that are physically and mentally connected, or I would rather say scientifically as we arise from the dark to see the light, visible light of the sun, the light that the human eye cannot perceive, it appears to be colourless, which we call white. Although we can see it, but white isn’t considered part of the visible spectrum or the way we see hardships caving a path for an extraordinary future. That’s because white light isn’t the light of a single colour but instead many colours, instead amalgamation of hard work, self-discipline and your willingness to get things done.
